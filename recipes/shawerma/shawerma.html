Shawerma

This easy, healthy homemade chicken shawarma is a huge fan-favorite around here, for good reason! The secret is in the simple shawarma spice mixture, which makes all the difference.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Salt
  • Marination Sauce
  • Big Vertical Rotating Skewer
  1. Cut the Chicken and marinate overnight in the fridge
  2. Stack around the skewer and don't forget the flab of fat on the top
  3. Let is spin and cook
  4. Cut and eat baby!
